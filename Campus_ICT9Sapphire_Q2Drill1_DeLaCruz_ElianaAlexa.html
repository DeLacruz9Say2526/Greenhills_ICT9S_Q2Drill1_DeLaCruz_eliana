Emergency Go Bag Essentials

Emergency water and food

Keep atleast one liter of clean water for hydraation during emergencies. And perishable food items, food bars, and canned goods.

First Aid Kit

Make sure to pack basic medicqal supplies like bandages, antiseptic and pain releivers.

Flashlight and whistle

Pack a good flashlight to rely on when in the dark and for emergency signals. Pack a whistle to call for help when needed.

Extra Clothes

Bring a change of clothes to stay warm and dry. can also be used for bandaging a wound.
